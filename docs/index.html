Built for real washing days

Stop reading a full weather forecast just to decide on laundry.

A normal forecast tells you temperature, rain, wind, humidity, and cloud. Dry My Washing turns those signals into the question people actually ask: will the washing dry outside?

It is designed for quick laundry decisions in changeable weather, especially in places like New Zealand, Australia, the UK, Ireland, and the Pacific Northwest where the sky can look fine while rain is still moving in later.

Quick answers

Common laundry weather questions.

Can washing dry outside today?

It can if there is enough dry time, useful wind, manageable humidity, and no rain arriving too soon.

What weather dries clothes fastest?

Dry, mild to warm air with a steady breeze usually matters more than sun alone.

What if rain is due later?

Dry My Washing helps you spot the safe drying window and Pro can warn you when washing is out.

Is Dry My Washing free?

The app is free to download, with optional Pro features for washing-out tracking and rain alerts.
