Dry My Washing Support

Need help with Dry My Washing, found something that looks wrong, or have an idea for a future update? Send a note and I will take a look.

Email support Privacy policy

What to include

  • Your iPhone model and iOS version
  • Your location or nearest town
  • What the app showed and what you expected instead
  • A screenshot if you have one

Common help topics

  • If the weather looks wrong, include the location shown in the app.
  • If an alert did not arrive, check notification permissions for Dry My Washing in iOS Settings.
  • If Pro features are not available after purchase, try restoring purchases from the app.

About the app

Dry My Washing helps you decide whether your washing will dry outside today by turning weather conditions into a simple drying window, drying score, and rain risk.
