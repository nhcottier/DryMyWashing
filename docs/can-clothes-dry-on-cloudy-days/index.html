Laundry weather guide

Can clothes dry on cloudy days?

Yes, clothes can dry on cloudy days. They dry when moisture evaporates, and that can still happen without direct sun if the air is dry and moving.

The real problem is when cloudy weather also brings high humidity, low wind, cool air, or showers. Dry My Washing checks those signals together.

Cloudy can still work when

  • There is a steady breeze.
  • Humidity is not too high.
  • Rain is unlikely for several hours.
  • You have enough time before evening cooling.

Check before you hang it out

Open Dry My Washing to see whether a cloudy day is still useful laundry weather or whether the washing is better kept inside.
