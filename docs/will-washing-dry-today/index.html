Laundry weather guide

Will washing dry today?

The short answer depends on sun, wind, humidity, cloud, and how soon rain arrives. A warm day is helpful, but washing can still dry slowly if the air is damp or still.

Dry My Washing is built for this exact question. It reads the forecast and gives you a simple answer before you hang clothes outside.

Quick checks

  • Look for a few dry hours in a row.
  • Light wind helps move moisture away from clothes.
  • High humidity slows drying even when it is not raining.
  • Rain later in the day means you need a safe bring-in time.

Use Dry My Washing

The app shows the best drying window today, a drying score, expected drying time, and rain risk so you can decide quickly.

Will washing dry today if it is cloudy?
It can, as long as the air is dry enough, there is some wind, and rain stays away for long enough.
