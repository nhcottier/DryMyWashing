Laundry weather guide

Best weather to dry clothes outside

The best weather to dry clothes outside is dry, mild to warm, and lightly breezy. Full sun is great, but wind and low humidity can matter just as much.

Dry My Washing checks those conditions together and shows the best drying window today.

Best conditions

  • Several hours without rain.
  • Low to moderate humidity.
  • A gentle breeze across the line.
  • Enough daylight or warmth for heavier items.

When to skip the line

If rain is close, the air is very damp, or the good weather ends too soon, it may be better to wait. Dry My Washing makes that call easier.
