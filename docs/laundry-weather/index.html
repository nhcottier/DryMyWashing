Laundry weather guide

Laundry weather

Laundry weather is the kind of weather that helps washing dry outside. It is not just sunny weather. Good laundry weather is dry air, enough warmth, useful breeze, low rain risk, and enough daylight left.

Dry My Washing turns laundry weather into a simple drying score and a practical window for your location.

What matters most

  • Sun and warmth help evaporate moisture.
  • Wind is useful when it is not too strong.
  • Humidity is often the hidden reason clothes stay damp.
  • Cloud can be fine if the air is dry and moving.
